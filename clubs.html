CLUBS AND SOCITIES

ST.ANTHONY'S BOYS HIGH SCHOOL BAND (M.I.B) MEN IN BRASS

The St.Anthony's Boys High School band was established in 2008 by Mr. John Lugaka former music teacher at AHS now at St Mary's Academy. It has now since formed as integral part of the school community. Functions are;

  1. Leading in the national anthem on Mondays, Fridays and national holidays.
  2. Leading hymns on band Tuesday
  3. Performing during major school events.
  4. Representing the school in the national school festivals.
  5. Offering musical accompaniment to the scouts.

GOLF CLUB

This is one of the newest clubs to be introduced in the school. The club has enabled us to go to a number of golf clubs such as Muthaiga and Karen golf clubs.

All this has been able to happen because of the old boys association which has donated some money to finance some of our equipment. Furthermore in the upcoming year we intend to hold tournaments with schools such as Oshwal and Braeburn. We hope to seek a lot of support to enhance our training and generally the support and become rather better in golf.
